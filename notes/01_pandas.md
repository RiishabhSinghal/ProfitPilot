Why can't an LLM directly read an Excel file?
Because excel files are binary data. They can't be directly read by llm.

What is a DataFrame?
It is a 2-d structered way to present data in rows and columns.

What is schema inference?
It is process of inferring or understanding the schema of the data.

Why do we perform a Data Audit before analysis?
It is first important to understand the data. We need to check for missing values, data types, count , distribution etc.
